Methodology of The PenTrix

The PenTrix follows a structured methodology inspired by industry standards like PTES (Penetration Testing Execution Standard), ensuring each phase from information gathering to reporting is approached with precision, ethics, and real-world application.

Pre-Engagement

Define the scope, goals, and legal boundaries before beginning the pentesting process.

Info Gathering & Enumeration

Collect data on the target systems and identify active services, ports, and potential entry points.

Vulnerability Scanning

Scan the target for known vulnerabilities using automated tools and manual analysis techniques.

Exploitation

Gain unauthorized access to systems or data by exploiting identified vulnerabilities during testing.

Post-Exploitation

Assess the value of the compromised system, maintain access, and determine the potential business impact.

Reporting

Document all findings, exploited vulnerabilities, and provide recommendations to strengthen security posture.
